# **App Name**: Aris AI Therapy

## Core Features:

- Initial Assessment: Gathers initial information about the user's mental state, concerns, and goals for therapy, acting as Dr. Aris with the defined persona and communication guidelines.
- Personalized Questioning: Uses Socratic questioning techniques tailored to the user's specific concerns to guide them toward their own insights, driven by the AI Dr. Aris persona.
- Cognitive Distortion Identification: Identifies and addresses cognitive distortions (e.g., catastrophizing, black-and-white thinking) using psychological frameworks.  The LLM will use reasoning as a tool to determine when a cognitive distortion is occuring.
- Progress Tracking: Allows users to track their progress over time, noting improvements in mood, behavior, and thought patterns.
- Resource Provision: Provides access to mental health resources and support networks.
- Crisis Intervention: Detects mentions of self-harm or harm to others and immediately provides crisis resources and encourages professional help, adhering to safety protocols.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calm light blue (#A0D2EB), promoting a sense of tranquility and openness.
- Background color: Soft, desaturated light blue (#EBF4FA) creating a peaceful and unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted lavender (#B39DDB), adding a touch of warmth and balance without overwhelming the senses.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif that lends an elegant, intellectual feel conducive to reflection.
- Use minimalist, line-based icons to represent emotions, resources, and progress, in the style of contemporary UX design.
- Ensure a clean and spacious layout with generous white space to minimize visual clutter and promote focus.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth transitions and micro-interactions to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
